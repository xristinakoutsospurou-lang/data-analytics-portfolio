# Business Insights

## Project Overview

This project analyzes marketing campaign performance across multiple brands, channels, customer segments, and campaign types.

The objective is to identify performance drivers, evaluate marketing efficiency, and generate actionable business recommendations.

---

# Key Findings

## 1. Channel Performance

Email achieved the highest average ROI among individual marketing channels, indicating strong cost efficiency.

Instagram generated the highest total revenue, highlighting strong customer engagement and conversion potential.

### Recommendation:
A combined strategy focusing on Email efficiency and Instagram reach could improve overall marketing performance.

---

## 2. Customer Segment Analysis

College Students generated the highest total revenue among customer segments.

Working Women showed strong ROI performance, indicating efficient campaign targeting.

Premium Shoppers showed the lowest average ROI and may require further campaign optimization.

### Recommendation:
Prioritize high-performing segments while investigating opportunities to improve Premium Shopper engagement.

---

## 3. Brand Performance

Nykaa achieved the strongest overall performance:

- Highest revenue
- Highest conversions
- Highest average ROI

Tira achieved the lowest CPA, indicating efficient customer acquisition.

### Recommendation:
Use Nykaa's strategy as a benchmark while analyzing Tira's acquisition efficiency.

---

## 4. Campaign Type Performance

Paid Ads achieved the highest average ROI among campaign types.

Social Media campaigns also showed strong performance.

Influencer campaigns had the lowest ROI compared with other campaign types.

### Recommendation:
Increase focus on high-performing paid campaigns while optimizing influencer strategies.

---

## 5. Monthly Performance Trends

Performance remained relatively stable throughout the analyzed period.

The highest ROI months were:

- February 2025
- July 2024
- December 2024

June 2025 contains fewer records compared with previous months, suggesting partial period coverage.

### Recommendation:
Consider seasonal patterns when planning future campaign budgets.

---

## 6. Top Performing Campaigns

Highest ROI campaigns were mainly associated with:

- Tier 2 City Customers
- Paid Ads
- Multi-channel strategies

Successful combinations included:

- Google + Instagram
- Email + YouTube + Facebook
- Facebook + Google

### Recommendation:
Expand testing of successful channel combinations across similar customer segments.

---

# Overall Business Recommendation

Marketing performance is driven by the combination of:

- Effective channel selection
- Customer segmentation
- Campaign optimization
- Brand-specific strategies

Future budget allocation should prioritize channels and segments demonstrating higher ROI while continuously testing new campaign combinations.
